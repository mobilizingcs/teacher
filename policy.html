Policy The class setup privilege (required for setup of your own students/classes) comes with more responsibility in the Mobilize system. Please read the following and confirm your understanding

  • The user will not share his/her account username and password with anyone under any circumstances. A member of the Mobilize project will never ask the user for their password.
  • The user will create classes and add students only for the purpose of the Mobilize units and will not create classes for other teachers.
  • The user will ensure they are logged out of their account before leaving their computer unattended.
  • The user will keep generated accounts and passwords for students private and share the account information to assigned students only.
  • Mobilize personnel reserve the right to modify or terminate accounts of users at any time for any reason.
